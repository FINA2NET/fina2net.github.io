Project Manager
Company FINA LLC announces a vacancy for the position of Project Manager

Responsibilities:
  • Management and responsibility of implementation for IT projects including technical implementation of IT solutions.
  • Independent planning and implementation of national/local and international IT projects.
  • Project management and consultation within the technology area of profession.
  • Planning and implementation of IT solutions through analytical descriptive data and procedure, including communication structures and tools.

Requirements:
  • Strong written and spoken Russian and English language skills.
  • Knowledge of MS Project, Jira, Confluence, Visio, Excel, Word and PowerPoint.
  • Preferable experience of managing Database.
  • Knowledge of both theoretical and practical aspects of project management.

We offer you:
  • Personal and professional development.
  • Working with members of the qualified Agile team.
  • Opportunity to work on multilateral projects with international and prestigious organizations. (World Bank, USAID, UNDP etc.)
  • Attractive salary and positive working atmosphere.
