General Vacancies/Interns
LLC FINA hires only the best. People who thrive in a culture of innovation and empowerment. People who are active learners and have a positive attitude. People who are leaders and know that by working together we can run faster, reach higher and achieve more.

We are looking for a creative persons, who are interested in joining our team as an interns. This is a paid internship where you will gain in-depth skills in our tech stack that will take you further to success. It provides students a valuable experience with real world applications while being able to work a part-time, flexible schedule to fit your educational needs.

What We Look For:
  • You must be able to commute to our office during the internship
  • Ability to quickly learn new technologies
  • Have a natural curiosity to dig into a problem or task until completed
During your internship, you will be mentored by a senior members of the LLC FINA team and receive regular feedback as to your performance highlighting your successes and potential areas for growth.

Based on company needs and your evaluation, you may be considered for continuing internship or full-time employment opportunities upon graduation.

  • JavaScript Developer;
  • System Integrator;

  • Linux Administrator;
