DKMS

Dynamic Knowledge Management System

Core Objectives

  • Collect environmental data in an integrated and structured way
  • Ensure public participation and access to justice in line with the spirit of Aarhus Convention
  • Turn information into knowledge via pattern recognition
  • Support viable environmental impact assessment based on true data
  • Strengthen informed decision making in environmental policy

Functionality of DKMS

DKMS turns collected information streams into reusable knowledge domains, thus improving decision making, collaboration, emergency response. DKMS is fully customizable for sensor, citizen, location, financial data acquisition with Intuitive user interface and customizable reporting. DKMS is perfect tool for Government Agencies and/or private Enterprises in Environmental, Agricultural, Emergency, Forestry policy fields.
